How do I place an order?
Here’s how you can place an order:
1) Select a catalog that you want to order
2) Within catalog, select the specific product
3) On product details page, click on 'ADD TO CART'
4) Now, in pop-up, select a Size and Quantity and press 'Done'
5) Next, you need to visit your cart by clicking on the cart button (top right corner)
6) Choose a payment method
7) Review order details and click on 'PROCEED'
8) Here, add the delivery address and click on 'PROCEED'
9) In sender section, add your name and contact no., and click on 'PROCEED'
10) Next, enter the final amount that you want to charge the customer
11) Click 'PAY NOW'
How do I search for a product?
There are two ways to search for products:
i.Through Image Code: All the product images on our App have a unique code on the bottom left corner. To search,
1) Go to 'For You' section
2) Click on the search icon on the top right corner
3) Enter the image code
ii. Through Catalog Name or Category: You can follow the same steps as above and search for a product using catalog name or category, instead of image code.
iii. Through My Shared Catalogs: You can also search for those catalogs that you have already shared with your customers. You will find this option in the Account section.

How do I make a payment using COD?
Here’s how you can make a payment using COD:
1) Add a product to your cart
2) Go to the Cart section
3) Here, you’ll see list of payment options
4) Select ‘Cash on Delivery’ as the payment mode
5) Enter Shipping and Sender’s address
6) Enter the final customer amount and click on 'PAY NOW'
Note: In case delivery executive has collected extra amount than the order value OR the order is marked delivered but your customer did not receive it, please give us a call or drop an email within 48 hours of order delivery, failing which the claim will not be entertained.
What are the different modes of making a payment?
We offer multiple payment modes. The list includes:
1) COD
2) Credit/Debit card
3) Netbanking
4) Multiple Wallets
5) UPI
6) Phonepe
7) Paytm
8) Gpay
